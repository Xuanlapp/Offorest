# Sticker Select Only After Master Summary

- Da cap nhat logic chon nhieu item trong Sticker page chi hoat dong khi item da co ket qua `2. CREATE MASTER` (`base64`).
- Checkbox tung dong bi disable neu chua co master image va hien nhan `Tao master truoc`.
- Nut `Update da chon` chi duoc bat khi co it nhat 1 item vua duoc chon va da san sang update.
- Da them bo dem `San sang update` de phan biet so item da chon va so item co the update ngay.
- Da them co che tu dong bo chon item neu master image bi mat/loi de tranh update sai.
